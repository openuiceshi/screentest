Technical Details: The Color Green

  • Hex Code: #00FF00 This is the standard digital green, maximizing the green channel (255) while keeping red and blue at 0.
  • Why Green for VFX? Digital camera sensors are most sensitive to green, capturing more detail and less noise in this channel. That's why green screens are the industry standard for keying.

Green Screen FAQs

Can I use this for Zoom backgrounds?

Yes. If you don't have a physical green cloth, you can display this page on a large TV or monitor behind you to help Zoom replace your background more accurately.

What if I see a black dot?

That is likely a dead green sub-pixel. It means that specific part of the pixel cannot light up.

What is the hexadecimal color code for green?

The hexadecimal color code for green is #00FF00. This tool displays that exact color value across the entire screen for consistent visual reference.

How does a green screen differ from a white screen?

A white screen provides a neutral background commonly used for general testing or cleaning, while a green screen (#00FF00) displays a highly saturated green color suited for visual testing, vivid backgrounds, and basic green screen applications.
